## 1. Introduction
SmartKhata+ is a lightweight invoice and credit management system for small vendors.  
It allows vendors to create invoices, track customer dues, manage payments, and view sales analytics.

## 2. Problem Statement
Most small vendors maintain credit (khata) manually, leading to inaccuracies and loss.  
SmartKhata+ digitizes billing, payments, and credit tracking in a simple and reliable way.

## 3. Goals
- Simple billing for vendors
- Accurate credit/owe tracking
- Manage customers and products
- Support partial and full payments
- Monthly and daily reports
- Admin-level analytics

## 4. Scope
Included:
- Invoice generation
- Owe/credit ledger
- Customer management
- Product management
- Payments recording
- Analytics reports

Excluded (future):
- Inventory automation
- Payment gateway integration

## 5. High-Level Summary
Frontend: React  
Backend: Spring Boot  
Database: PostgreSQL  
Deployment: Docker & CI/CD
